What is Persist?

Pledge money.

Control habits.

Use Persist.

Here's the idea: You pledge money to a select charity of your choice, choose your end date and then commit. Persist until your end date and the money is returned.

Persist works. Negative reinforcement can sound menacing but when you are trying to shake off that 8 year smoking addiction or are simply trying to eat healthier everyday, there needs to be additional reasons to continue.
